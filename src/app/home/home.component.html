Ed Skoviak Computing (ESC) is an advisory practice, dedicated to helping companies establish an Enterprise Architecture practice. We believe that this practice is most successful if it is built organically within the company's Ethos. This means that that our practicioners partner with your senior IT staff to find and coach individuals in you exsiting organization to create the basis of your architectural Center of Excellence.

ESC defines Enterprise Architecture as the holistic view of the Information Technology (IT) stack in a company. Our approach blends the philosophy of a focus on the traditional TOGAF concept of Business, Data and Architecture (BDA) with the uniqueness of a firm's IT structure.

The IT systems in a compmany grow organically perhaps due to:

  • Expansion of a business capability
  • New business capability arises
  • Merger/Acquisition
  • Market Base growth
